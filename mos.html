Mean-Opinion Score (MOS) Test

Measuring quality of converted singing voice.

Thank you so much for participating in this test!

Listen to the sample of computer generated singing voice and assess the quality of the audio based on how close it is to real singing recordings. For better results, please wear headphones and stay in a quite environment. For each audio, please select an option below to indicate its quality: (本次测试要对计算机生成的歌唱音频做音质上的评估。该评估是判断生成的音频有多接近真实的歌唱录音数据。 请受试者在安静的环境下，使用耳机聆听，并根据每个音频的品质，给予以下几种评分：）

  • １（Unsatisfactory） Very annoying, distortion is objectionable.
  • 1.5 (in-between Unsatisfactory and Poor)
  • ２（Poor） Annoying distortion, but not objectionable.
  • 2.5 (in-between Poor and Fair)
  • ３（Fair） Perceptible distortion, slightly annoying.
  • 3.5 (in-between Fair and Good)
  • ４（Good） Slight perceptible level of distortion, but not annoying.
  • 4.5 (in-between Good and Excellent)
  • ５（Excellent） Imperceptible level of distortion.
In total, there are 6 compared systems; thus each group has 6 audios. In each group, samples from compared systems are randomly shuffled and a real singing recording is also included. We suggest you first listen to all audio samples in each group and then give scores for better comparison. You are allowed to replay samples as you want. Please remember to click the "Submit" botton after finishing. (一共有6个对比系统，所以每组实验有6个音频做对比；在每组中，各个系统的音频是随机排列的，其中有一个是真实的歌唱录音； 建议受试者可以先听完6个音频后，再根据彼此对比给出相应的分数；受试者可以重复听任何一句音频； 在结束之前，请点击 "Submit" 按钮。) NB:
