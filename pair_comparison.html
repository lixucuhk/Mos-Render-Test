Speaker Similarity Test

Measuring whether two audios belong to the same person.

Thank you so much for participating in this test!

In this test, we have computer-converted singing audios and real singing recordings. Each question has two audios. Please listen the two audios and answer the question based on the speaker similarity of the two audios (Only consider the speaker similarity; Audio quality, content and pitch are not taken into account).

(在这个实验中，我们有机器生成的歌唱音频和真实的歌唱录音；每个问题下有2个音频，请判断2个音频的说话者相似度。在此，我们仅考虑说话者相似度，不考虑音频的音质、内容和音高等其他信息。)

Options:

  • 確定是同一個語者 (same, absolutely sure)
  • 不大確定，但傾向認為是同一個語者 (same, not sure)
  • 不大確定，但傾向認為是不同語者 (different, not sure)
  • 確定是不同語者 (different, absolutely sure)

Please remember to click the "Submit" botton after finishing.

(在结束之前，请点击 "Submit" 按钮。)
